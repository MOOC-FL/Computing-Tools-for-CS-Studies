#### UKK
- On this page you can read clarifications on problems you may encounter during the course.
- [ ] **How do I get a grade for a course?**
- After a successfully completed exam, performance marks are transferred to SISU every few weeks.
- [ ] **My course completion is only shown as partial completion in Sisu.**
- Check that your study plan contains the correct year version of the course.
- [ ] **I just finished the exam but didn't get a grade.**
- Grades are only transferred every few weeks. This requires a bit of manual work.
- [ ] **I need a grade quickly to graduate.**
- The course is intended to be taken at the beginning of your studies. This course should not be the last course before graduation. If this is the case, please contact your instructor directly after you have successfully completed your exam.
